Meet Our Instructors

Kristen V.

Kristen is a compassionate yoga instructor who enjoys teaching creative and challenging flows for all levels. As a certified wildlife biologist, Kristen often incorporates themes of nature into her practice. She creates a warm and encouraging environment where students can take up space and express themselves unapologetically. Upon completing her 200-hour yoga teacher training with Yoga District in 2024, Kristen continued her education in guided meditation and mindfulness. She aims to build her students' confidence, inner peace, and self-love on and off the mat.
